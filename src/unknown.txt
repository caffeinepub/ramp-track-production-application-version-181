## Troubleshooting

### "App not installed" Error
- Enable "Install unknown apps" for your browser
- Ensure you have enough storage space
- Try downloading again (file may be corrupted)

### "For security, your phone is not allowed to install unknown apps"
- Go to Settings → Security → Unknown sources
- Enable for your browser or file manager

### App Won't Open
- Check you have Android 8.0 or higher
- Ensure internet connection (first launch requires network)
- Try clearing app data: Settings → Apps → Ramp Track → Clear data

### Camera/GPS Not Working
- Grant permissions: Settings → Apps → Ramp Track → Permissions
- Enable Camera and Location
